About Agriculture Aggregate

CS257 Winter 2021 Final Project, Kai Johnson and Antonia Ritter


What gets displayed?

If All countries is selected, a map is displayed. If not...

If All years is selected, a line graph is diplayed. If not...

If All crops is selected, a bar chart is displayed. If not...

a single production number is displayed.

For example:

All countries, All crops, and 2012 shows a map of total crop production in 2012.

Canada, All crops, and 2012 shows a bar chart of top crops produced in Canada in 2012.


Notes:

The data comes from the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations.

The crops shown in the multi-line graph (displayed when one country, all crops, and all years is selected) are those which produced the most in total, summed over all the years.
