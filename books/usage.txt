Usage and help statment for Books CLI
CS257 
Martin Bernard and Antonia Ritter
Jan 11 2021 

Arguments (order is irrelevant): 
	-a, --author: Search authors, in quotes if multiple words. (case insensitive)
	-t, --title: Search titles, in quotes if multiple words. (case insensitive) 
	-y, --year: Search year published. Two full years separated by hyphen or a single year.(inclusive)
	-h, --help: Prints usage help statments
	no arguments: Prints this page  
	
Examples: 
	python3 books.py --author Mary
		Mary Shelley
			Frankenstein, 1818
		Mary Higgins Clark
			A Stranger is Watching, 1977
			The Cinderella Murder, 2014

	python3 books.py -a "James Baldwin"
		James Baldwin
			The Fire Next Time, 1963
			If Beale Street Could Talk, 1974

	python3 books.py -t the
		Agatha Christie
			Murder on the Orient Express, 1934
			Death on the Nile, 1937
		L. Frank Baum
			The Wonderful Wizard of OZ, 1900 
		
	python3 books.py -a Agatha -t the 
	(or python3 books.py -t “the” -a “Agatha”) 
		Agatha Christie
			Murder on the Orient Express, 1934
			Death on the Nile, 1937
		
	python3 books.py -y 1800-1900
		Mary Shelley
			Frankenstein, 1818
		L. Frank Baum
			The Wonderful Wizard of OZ, 1900 
			
	python3 books.py
		prints this page
